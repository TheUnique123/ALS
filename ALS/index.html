Our Aim

Our aim is to spread awareness about animals that are endangered and persuade people about saving them and caring them. So, we assembled a team together to help save as much lives as we could in order to protect animals against unfair slaughters. We mainly focus on 3 types of animals, Marine, Wild life & Strays.
